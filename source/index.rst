=========
vCDAT 2.0
=========

**VCDAT2.0 currently under development.** Check out the :doc:`dev_coding_guidelines`, and :doc:`dev_install` pages.

vCDAT 2.0 is a JupyterLab extension specifically designed to combine the powerful vCDAT 1.0 suite of visualization tools with the interactive and collaborative environment offered by JupyterLab and JpuyterHub.

*This project is in the first stage of development. As such it is only a simple working demo that shows the basic concept and potential of the project.*
